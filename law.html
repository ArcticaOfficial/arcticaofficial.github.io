Laws Regarding Trains and Rail (01)

01/01: The max speed limit on all rail in arctica is 150kmph unless otherwise spefied.

01/02A: Rail CANNOT be connected or built without permission from the state.

01/02B: If the rail passes though multiple states you must get permission from the states aswell as the president.

01/03: Track must have ballast under it surrounded with gravel.

Laws Regarding Electricity and The National Grid (02)

02/01: The National Grid can only be connected to if the connector has payed the monthly bill.

02/02A: Any power supply connected to the national grid must be two way and both receive and send power to the national grid.

02/02B: If a one-way connection to the grid is found the connection will be terminated.
